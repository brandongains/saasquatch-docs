Usage-Based Tiers

How do you define a member?

A member is an identified person registered in your customer referral program. To determine the right tier for your business, we suggest you add up the total number of monthly active customers you currently have. The number resets each month so you don't get charged for inactive members.

Pricing Example

I’m a marketing director looking to buy a referral program for my company that has 25,000 active users. Given that usage you would select the Pro Plan, which would cost $1,550 per month. (Base of $800 + $750 at $50/1,000 users)
